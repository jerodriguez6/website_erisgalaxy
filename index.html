GAMEPLAY

2 VS 2 VS 2 VS GRIMER

A more promising mode where there will be three pairs of users fighting each other, also adding the Grimer that will be stalking them. This mode, unlike the previous one, is easier for the Grimer, who will be waiting for the pairs of races to confront each other, so that he can ambush the winner (who will probably be weakened). A little more adrenaline for the races, who must each play with an ally and fight against two other duos, and always taking into account the possibility of the Grimer appearing.

ALL VS GRIMER

A simple battle mode, where the six race users must kill the Grimer and ensure that the ship does not lose its functionality. This mode is going to be the easiest for the races, and the most dynamic for the Grimer player. From the point of view of the races, it is a game mode adapted to have fun and have a good time without having to be so alert, unlike other modes.

ALL VS ALL

In this game mode each user will play individually, being a challenge for everyone equally. Users must know how to use their resources, skills and be careful with their movements. It is not a mode for newbies, entering here is for those people who want to show what they are made of.
